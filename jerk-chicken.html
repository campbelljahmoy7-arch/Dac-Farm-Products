DAC Signature Family Jerk Chicken

Family-friendly spice • smoky char • juicy inside • “lick your fingers” good

Ingredients (Serves 4–6)

  • 3–4 lbs chicken (legs & thighs recommended)
  • 2 tbsp DAC Green Seasoning
  • 1 tsp Scotch Bonnet Pepper Powder (use 1/2 tsp for milder heat)
  • 1 tbsp thyme (or 1 tsp dried)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp allspice (pimento)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl to form a thick paste.
  2. Pat chicken dry and make 2–3 shallow cuts in each piece.
  3. Rub marinade all over and into the cuts. Cover and refrigerate.
  4. Marinate at least 4 hours (overnight is best).
  5. Grill: Medium heat, 35–45 mins, turn every 7–8 mins for a smoky char.
  6. Oven: Bake 375°F (190°C) for 40 mins, then broil 5–7 mins for color/char.
  7. Rest 5 mins before serving.
Pro Finish (restaurant look):
Mix 1 tbsp DAC Green Seasoning + 1 tbsp oil + a tiny pinch of Pepper Powder. Drizzle lightly over the hot chicken before serving.

Serve With

  • Rice & peas
  • Festival
  • Fried plantain
  • Coleslaw
